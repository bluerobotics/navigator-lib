Expand description

The Oklab color space.

Oklab is a perceptually-uniform color space similar in structure to L*a*b*, but tries to have a better perceptual uniformity. It assumes a D65 whitepoint and normal well-lit viewing conditions.

Fields§

§l: T

L is the lightness of the color. 0 gives absolute black and 1 gives the brightest white.

§a: T

a goes from red at -1 to green at 1.

§b: T

b goes from yellow at -1 to blue at 1.
